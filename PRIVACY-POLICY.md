# Privacy Policy — WordBloom

**Last updated:** June 24, 2026

## Overview

WordBloom is a browser-based word puzzle game. Your privacy is important to us.
This policy explains what data the game handles and how.

## Data Collection

**WordBloom does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

All game data (your current level, coin balance, and sound preference) is stored
locally in your browser using the standard `localStorage` API. This data never
leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

## What We Don't Do

- We do **not** collect any personal or identifying information
- We do **not** track your browsing activity
- We do **not** transmit any data to external servers
- We do **not** use analytics, telemetry, or tracking scripts
- We do **not** use cookies
- We do **not** require account creation or sign-in

## Local Data Stored

| Key | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| `wordbloom.save.v1` | Current level, coin balance, mute setting, and whether the intro was shown |

You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser's site data for the page.

## Third-Party Services

WordBloom runs entirely in your browser and bundles its own word list. It makes
no third-party network requests during gameplay. If you host it on a platform
such as Vercel or GitHub Pages, that host's own privacy policy applies to basic
request logs.
